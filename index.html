About Fortnite

Fortnite is more than just a battle royale — it's a living, ever-evolving universe. With V-Bucks, the in-game currency, players unlock thousands of cosmetics, emotes, gliders, pickaxes, and exclusive skins from iconic collaborations (Marvel, Star Wars, anime, musicians, and more). The Item Shop refreshes daily, bringing new limited-time items and keeping the game fresh and exciting for millions worldwide.

Fast-paced building and editing Zero Build mode for pure shooting action

The heart of Fortnite lies in its intense combat. Master building and editing to outplay opponents in classic Battle Royale, or jump straight into gunfights with Zero Build mode. Weapons, mobility items, vehicles, and mythic abilities create endless possibilities — from sniper duels at 200 meters to chaotic close-range build battles. Every match feels different, every victory earned through skill, strategy, and split-second decisions.
